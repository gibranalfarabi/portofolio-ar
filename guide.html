Guide

Follow these steps to start Augmented Reality simulation:

  1. Download Image Marker
    Navigate to the "Marker" page within the app to find and download the image markers required for the AR experience
  2. Print or Display Image Markers
    You can either print the downloaded markers or display them on another screen. Ensure that the markers are clear and well-lit for optimal performance.
  3. Point Your Camera at the Marker
    Open the AR view within the app and point your device's camera at one of the image markers.
  4. Click the "Start AR"
    Navigate to the "Start AR" page within the app to start scanning.
  5. Allow Camera Access
    When you first launch the application, you will be prompted to allow access to your device's camera. This permission is necessary for the AR features to function properly. Please grant the access.
  6. Interact and Enjoy
